To integrate the backend with the frontend, you first need to understand
that they are separate parts of your application.The magic that brings them together is
the API (Application Programming Interface).
APIs act as intermediaries that allow communication between the frontend and the backend .
When a user interacts with your web app, the frontend sends requests to the backend through the API,
and the backend processes those requests, sending back the necessary data to display on the frontend.
It's like a bridge connecting the two parts!
